# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting comments, and personal or political attacks.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behaviour may be reported to the project maintainers via the repository's
Security tab or a private issue. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
